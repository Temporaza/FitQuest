INTRODUCTION

Obesity is now the most common nutritional disorder infancy and childhood in this country and probably in the world.

Childhood obesity and sedentary lifestyle | First-time parents who lack knowledge when it comes to taking care of their little ones.

FitQuest Junior is a mobile and web application that is capable of giving tasks for kids based on the BMI status and helping parents to track their kids milestone.
GAMIFICATION

FitQuest Junior's "Gamification" feature enhances the fitness journey for kids through engaging game elements. It includes challenges, rewards, and interactive activities designed to motivate and inspire children to stay active and achieve their fitness goals in a fun and enjoyable way.

Overall, the "Gamification" feature in FitQuest Junior transforms fitness into a playful and rewarding experience, encouraging kids to stay active, healthy, and engaged.

It inspires a sense of excitement and accomplishment, encouraging children to discover the joy of staying active and healthy while having a blast along the way.

DIGITAL INFANT CARE GUIDE

Your duty as a parent in nurturing a child is important comes with many challenges. FitQuest Junior remains your partner in your journey toward your child’s growth and development.

This health book contains guides and tips that can help you take care of your child as they grow. This can also be a tool that you and your Pediatrician can use to take note of reminders during regular checkups and consultations.

Baby eBook

The recommendations in this health eBook are intended only as a guide for both parents/caregivers and the practitioners in the conduct of well-baby/child checkups, anticipatory care, and preventive healthcare, including immunization. These recommendations MUST NOT be regarded as absolute but as supplementary or complementary information only.

TASK RECOMMENDER

FitQuest Junior's "Task Recommender" feature suggests daily activities tailored to each child's age, fitness level, and preferences. It helps kids stay engaged and motivated by providing personalized tasks that contribute to their overall health and well-being.

Not only suggests daily activities but also rewards kids with points for completing tasks. These points can be used to unlock and purchase accessories that match their preferences, adding a fun and personalized touch to their fitness journey.
